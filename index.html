About Me

Hello! I'm Renalou Cudo, a graphic design student who enjoys creating simple and creative visual designs. I started learning design during my college years, where I discovered my interest in turning ideas into visuals such as posters, social media graphics, and basic branding materials.

As a student, I am continuously improving my skills by practicing design projects, exploring different design styles, and learning new tools. I enjoy experimenting with colors, layouts, and typography to create designs that are both visually appealing and easy to understand.

When I'm not designing, I spend time learning from online resources and getting inspiration from other designers. I am eager to grow, learn, and gain more experience in the field of graphic design.
